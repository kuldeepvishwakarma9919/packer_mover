Ultra-Shift Pro ("hum", "humein", ya "hamara") aapki privacy ka pura samman karta hai. Yeh policy batati hai ki jab aap hamari website use karte hain, toh hum aapka data kaise collect aur secure karte hain.

1. Hum Kaunsa Data Collect Karte Hain?

Jab aap quotation mangte hain ya humein contact karte hain, hum niche di gayi jankari collect kar sakte hain:

  • Pehchaan: Naam aur Surname.
  • Contact: Phone number aur Email address.
  • Shifting Details: Pick-up address aur Delivery address.
  • Technical: IP address aur Browser type (Analytics ke liye).

2. Aapka Data Kaise Use Hota Hai?

Aapka data sirf niche diye gaye maqsad ke liye use kiya jata hai:

  • Shifting ka accurate estimate (Price) dene ke liye.
  • Humaare supervisor ka survey schedule karne ke liye.
  • Aapko booking confirmation aur tracking updates bhejne ke liye.
  • Humaari services ko improve karne ke liye.

3. Data Security (Suraksha)

Humne aapke data ko unauthorized access se bachane ke liye strict physical aur electronic security measures lagaye hain. Hum aapka data kisi bhi third-party marketing agency ko NAHI BECHTE.

4. Cookies Ka Istemal

Hamari website cookies ka istemal karti hai taaki hum samajh sakein ki users ko kaunsi services zyada pasand aa rahi hain. Aap chahein toh apne browser setting se ise disable kar sakte hain.

5. Aapke Adhikar

Aapko pura haq hai ki aap humse puchein ki humne aapka kaunsa data store kiya hai, aur aap use kabhi bhi delete karne ki request kar sakte hain.
