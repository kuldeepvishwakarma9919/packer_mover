TERMS & POLICIES

Humari services use karne se pehle kripya inhe dhyan se padhein.

1. Service Agreement

Ultra-Shift Pro ke dwara di gayi har service customer aur company ke beech ek binding agreement hai. Shifting ki date fix karne ke liye 20% token amount dena anivarya hai jo final bill mein adjust ho jayega.

2. Insurance & Liability

Hum transit insurance provide karte hain. Iska claim tabhi valid hoga jab:

  • Packing hamari team ke dwara ki gayi ho.
  • Unloading ke waqt hi damage report kiya gaya ho.
  • Cash, Jewelry aur Personal Electronic items hamari liability mein nahi aate.
Note: Fragile items (Kaanch ka saaman) ke liye extra safety packing recommend ki jaati hai jiska charge alag se lag sakta hai.

3. Prohibited Items (Ban Items)

Suraksha ke dristikon se, hum niche likhe huye items transport NAHI karte:

Petroleums & Fuels
Gas Cylinders / Crackers
Live Plants (Long Distance)
Pets & Animals

4. Cancellation Policy

Agar aap plan cancel karte hain:

  • Shifting se 48 ghante pehle: 100% Refund
  • Shifting se 24 ghante pehle: 50% Refund
  • Shifting wale din: No Refund
